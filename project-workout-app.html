Glow AI - Mobile App

  • Home
  • Glow AI - Mobile App

Project Overview

This project focuses on designing a comprehensive fitness mobile application that helps users achieve their health and wellness goals through personalized workout plans, progress tracking, and motivational features. The goal was to create an engaging and intuitive interface that makes fitness accessible to users of all experience levels.

The design approach was centered around user motivation and habit formation, ensuring that the app provides clear guidance, celebrates achievements, and maintains user engagement through gamification elements and social features. By implementing modern UX principles such as intuitive workout tracking, visual progress indicators, and personalized recommendations, the app helps users stay consistent with their fitness routines.

The project also emphasizes accessibility and inclusivity, ensuring the design accommodates users with varying fitness levels, physical abilities, and technological comfort while maintaining a motivating and supportive user experience.

  • Fitness UX Design

  • Mobile First Design

  • UI / Visual Design

  • Gamification Features

  • Progress Tracking UX

Key Features & Results

Personalized workout plans based on fitness goals and experience level, detailed exercise library with video demonstrations, real-time workout tracking with timer and rep counting, progress analytics with visual charts, achievement system and milestone celebrations, social community features for motivation, nutrition tracking integration, and customizable workout scheduling with smart reminders.
